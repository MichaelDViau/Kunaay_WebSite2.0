Discover Casa Secreto Maya, a hidden jewel in the prestigious gated community of Playacar Phase 1. This stunning beachfront villa combines contemporary elegance with the tranquil beauty of the Caribbean, creating the perfect sanctuary for families, friends, or couples seeking an unforgettable escape.

Step through the doors and be greeted by breathtaking ocean views at every turn. The home features spacious living areas with soaring ceilings, refined finishes, and an open design that invites the sea breeze indoors. A gourmet kitchen, crafted with high-end materials, flows seamlessly into the dining and lounge spaces — ideal for both entertaining and relaxing.

Outdoors, the villa offers direct beach access, a private pool, and expansive terraces where you can bask in the sun or enjoy shaded gatherings. Every detail has been thoughtfully chosen to reflect the spirit of the Riviera Maya while offering modern luxury and comfort.
